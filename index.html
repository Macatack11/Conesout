Guidance Tool Only

ConesOut is a guidance and reference tool to assist trained utility workers in setting out signs, lights and guarding at road works.

This app does NOT replace formal training, a site-specific Risk Assessment, Method Statement, or a Traffic Management Plan approved by the relevant Highway Authority.

All distances and procedures are based on Chapter 8 of the Traffic Signs Manual, TSRGD 2016, NRSWA 1991 and TMA 2004 as guidance only.
